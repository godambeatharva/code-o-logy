About Event

Code-o-logy is a coding event organized under the banner of CSI-KGCE. It was a three day event comprising of various competitions related to the world of programming. Competition was open to all the Engineering Streams and studnets could compete in Open (for B.E. & T.E.) or Junior (for S.E.) class.

Main aim of the event was to familiarise students with different concepts of programmig such as Pseudocodes, Competitive Coding and Debugging (Bug Hunting). Participation was completely free for encourging student engagement. All the problem statements utilised during the event were curated by the student management members!
